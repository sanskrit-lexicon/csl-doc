=======================================
ACC Catalogus Catalogorum  (User notes)
=======================================

This  Alphabetical Register of Sanskrit Works and Authors is a 
reference work; it is not a general dictionary. Entries are names of
works or names of authors.  The entries in each volume are separately
alphabetized.  Text of the entries is in English.
