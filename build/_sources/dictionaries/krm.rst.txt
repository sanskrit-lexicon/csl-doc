KRM Kṛdantarūpamālā  (User notes)
=================================

This is a concordance of verbal derivatives.

The 'headwords' are the roots, with anubandhas.  

The most useful display for initial exploration might be
:doc:`../displays/list`.

The English Title and Prefaces from the five volumes are below
in this documentation.  The Sanskrit Front Matter may be obtained
from the krm_bookmark.pdf download.
